# Support at Home

**DXC Care Services** connects Microsoft Dynamics 365 directly to Services Australia's Support at Home APIs. Budget checks, funding claims, invoice submission, payments, and reconciliation — all inside D365, secured by PRODA, with a complete audit trail.

---

## How it works

```
Check funding position
        ↓
Raise the funding claim
        ↓
Get paid and reconcile
        ↓
Verify the loop is closed
```

Follow the four pages in order for the complete walkthrough.
