## Fixing Vendor EDI errors
The following table describes a few staging errors that could be experienced with vendor inbound documents at the staging to target step. Short description of possible fixes are discussed. After fix, reset status on the staging record and either manually process again or leave for batch to process.

Document                          | Error	          | How to fix
:--                               |:--              |:--
Vendor advanced shipping notice   | Purchase order x is no longer confirmed | Confirm the D365 purchase order
Vendor advanced shipping notice   | Insufficient open deliver remainder in purchase order lines for item x  | If quantity is acceptable: increase the purchase order line's deliver remainder or over delivery %
